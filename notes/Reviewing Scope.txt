Reviewing Scope

Got all that? Let’s review the rules on scope:
■ Local variables: In scope from declaration to the end of the block
■ Method parameters: In scope for the duration of the method
■ Instance variables: In scope from declaration until the object is eligible for garbage 
collection
■ Class variables: In scope from declaration until the program ends
Not sure what garbage collection is? Relax: that’s our next and final section for 
this chapter
